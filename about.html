"The book fair comeback"

The book fair returns to lima and in person for the entire peruvian public.

After a long time of being locked up due to the pandemic, the book fair returns to "el Parque de los Proceres" in the district of Jesus María where you will meet your favorite authors and writers in this event that takes place every year in the July

22 July, 2022

Lima, Perú

Literary Talent

Among the representatives of Spain, David Roas, a Spanish writer and literary critic, specializing in fantastic literature, stands out, who will share dialogue tables on literary criticism and crime novels. Once again, we are visited by Juan Casamayor, founder of Páginas de Espuma, an independent label that is a reference in the genre of short stories in Spanish.
